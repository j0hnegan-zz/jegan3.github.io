Prepare

Flexible On-Call Scheduling

Set up on-call users and teams in rotations and escalation policies based on time, alert severity, type of alert, or other custom factors. Choose from a range of contact methods (SMS, phone, push, email, chat). See who is on-call and active. Let users easily “tap out” of a scheduled shift.

Integrate With Your Existing Systems

VictorOps integrates with your monitoring tools, eventing or ticketing systems, chat and dev tools, acting as a centralized hub for routing alerts and serving as a source of record if you choose. People can stay in their tool of choice and still stay in communication.

Trusted Alert Delivery

VictorOps was built on clustered and highly redundant hardware and software. We have integrated with industry standard delivery services and offer multiple touch points to ensure you get alerted! Our private cloud infrastructure insulates us from large scale public cloud failures.

Respond

Powerful Incident Routing

Silence unactionable alerts, rank them by severity, and automatically route them to the right responder. VictorOps offers alert transformations that ensure that the right expert get on the case, and that nobody wakes up in the middle of the night unless there is a very good reason.

Enhanced Incident Context

Send remediation instructions, monitoring graphs, and other relevant information alongside alerts, so that responders can deal with the problem fast.

Postmortem Reporting and Metrics

VictorOps facilitates postmortem meetings by passively capturing the timeline of all communications and events. It serves as a black box for your infrastructure. Reports capture incident trends and key metrics such as MTTR, and team performance.

VictorOps Enterprise

API and Webhooks

Use VictorOps API calls to customize and extend functionality to meet your needs. Outbound webhooks extend platform data into other systems, allowing for everything from custom dashboarding to fixing standard recurring issues automatically.
